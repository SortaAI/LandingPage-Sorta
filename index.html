COMMON QUESTIONS

You asked. We'll be transparent with you.

Do I need to replace my EHR or practice management system?

No. Sorta is not an EHR. It lives entirely outside your existing software. Your current system stays exactly as-is — nothing changes for your clinical team, billing, or scheduling.

How long does setup take?

Upload your existing clinic forms once and you're ready. Most clinics are live the same day they sign up. No IT department. No migration. No training beyond "type here, click there."

Is patient data secure?

Yes. Data is processed securely and handled with HIPAA-appropriate practices. Each patient visit generates a timestamped, frozen chart snapshot — your unalterable audit trail.

What types of clinics is this for?

Independent outpatient clinics of any specialty — physical therapy, mental health, family medicine, ENT, optometry, chiropractic, and more. If you have 1–50 staff and deal with patient paperwork, this is for you.

Does it work with scanned paper forms or digital PDFs?

Both. Sorta handles flat scanned PDFs and digital AcroForm PDFs. Upload whatever your clinic already uses — the AI identifies the fields and converts it into a fillable template automatically.

What if I want to cancel?

No contracts, no cancellation fees. $300/month, cancel any time. We're confident you'll stick around once your front desk sees 40 minutes back in their day.
