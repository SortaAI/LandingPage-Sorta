PRICING FAQ

Pricing questions — clear, practical answers.

Why $300 a month?

Because that's what makes sense for an independent outpatient clinic. Enterprise intake platforms charge $500–$8,000/month because they were built for hospital systems. We were built for the clinic down the street. $300 is the price that lets you try it without a budget meeting.

Is there a per-patient fee?

No. $300/month covers unlimited patients, unlimited forms, unlimited visits. We don't charge more if you get busier.

What's included in the free pilot?

Everything. Full product access, your actual forms loaded and ready, a 20-minute onboarding walkthrough. 30 days free, no credit card required. After 30 days it's $300/month — or you cancel, no questions asked.

Do I need to sign a contract?

No. Month-to-month. Cancel anytime. We're confident you won't want to once your front desk sees the difference.

What happens if I want to cancel?

You cancel. No fees, no retention calls, no guilt trip. Your forms and templates stay in the system for 30 days in case you change your mind.

Is there a setup fee?

No. Setup is included. We onboard you, load your forms, and walk your front desk through it. One time, included in the $300/month.

What if I have more than one clinic location?

Each location is $300/month. Multi-location clinics get their own separate tenant — forms, patients, and data are fully isolated between locations.

When does Tier 2 — payer workflows — launch?

Q3 2026 is our target for pilots. If you're interested, join the waitlist through the demo form and we'll reach out when spots open.
