The paperwork problem that never goes away.

Nephrology intake is among the most documentation-heavy in outpatient medicine. A new patient brings a complex medical history, multiple medications, prior procedures, and insurance from multiple payers. Every piece of that information ends up on multiple forms.

ESRD patients on dialysis have recurring visits with largely the same demographic and insurance information. Your staff is retyping that same information at every visit because the forms don't talk to each other.

The clinical complexity of nephrology is unavoidable. The administrative complexity isn't. Sorta handles the paperwork layer so your staff can focus on the patient.

What changes when you use Sorta.

Complex history. Entered once.

Long medical histories, multiple medications, multiple payers — patients enter it all once on their phone. Every form gets it automatically.

Recurring visit memory

Returning patients confirm what changed. For patients with frequent visits, this alone saves significant front desk time every week.

HIPAA-eligible infrastructure

Patient data encrypted in transit and at rest. AI only runs at setup — never touches patient information at visit time.
